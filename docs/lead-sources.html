Tier 1 Use First

Highest-quality, lowest-friction sources. Start here. Apollo is the single biggest lever.

Apollo.io Paid — $49/mo Wired in dialer

URL
apollo.io
What you get
email, phone, LinkedIn, title, company, website, employee count, revenue range, industry, location
Filters that matter
title = Owner/President/CEO, employees 3–30, revenue $500k–$5M, industry = knowledge-work vertical
Volume
~1,000 Mikes/month for $49
How to use
Export CSV → drop into the dialer's CSV import panel. Apollo headers are auto-detected.

NPI Registry Free

URL
npiregistry.cms.hhs.gov
Bulk dump
download.cms.gov/nppes/NPI_Files.html — monthly full DB, ~8 GB CSV, ~8M providers
What you get
provider name, practice name, address, phone, taxonomy code (specialty)
Filter
solo/small-practice taxonomy codes: family medicine, internal medicine, dental, chiropractic, PT, mental health
Missing
email — need to enrich via Hunter.io or Apollo domain lookup
Volume
~500k filtered targets nationally

State Bar Associations Free

Texas
texasbar.com — Find a Lawyer
California
calbar.ca.gov — Licensee Search
New York
courts.state.ny.us — Attorney Search
Florida
floridabar.org — Find a Member
What you get
attorney name, firm name, address, bar-registered email (sometimes)
Volume
50k+ solos/smalls per major state
Note
filter by practice area (estate planning, family law, small biz) and firm size
No central directory — each state has its own site. Scrape one state at a time.

AICPA & State CPA Societies Free

AICPA main
us.aicpa.org/forthepublic/findacpa
State societies
each state has a "find a CPA" tool with firm name + location
Volume
~400k CPAs nationally; small practices are the target
Missing
email — enrich via Apollo

tdi.texas.gov/agent

NARPM Directory (Property Management) Free

Tier 2 Intent Signals

Free scraping of public intent signals — businesses actively broadcasting the pain you solve.

Indeed Job Postings Free High Intent

URL
indeed.com
Search terms
administrative assistant, office manager, intake coordinator, data entry in target city
Signal
any business hiring these roles is drowning in admin = Mike
Cold email hook
"noticed you posted for an admin assistant — at ~$42k loaded cost, that's $X/year..."
Build
~4–6 hours to wire a scraper into the dialer
Volume
200+ high-intent leads/month per major metro

BBB (Better Business Bureau) Free

URL
bbb.org/us/search
What you get
business name, address, phone, website, years in business, accreditation status, rating
Filter
accredited businesses in target categories
Note
anti-bot measures exist; scrape carefully

Chamber of Commerce Directories Free

Example
austinchamber.com/directory
Quality
very high — members pay annual dues, actively growing businesses
Volume
500–3,000 members per major city chamber
Effort
manual or custom scraper per chamber; doesn't scale well but the quality is unmatched
